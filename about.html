About Us

Excellence through process improvement since 1998

Quality Circle Institute (QCI)

We are a global consulting and training firm dedicated to organizational excellence through process improvement, ISO management systems, and digital transformation.

Our Vision

To be the catalyst for world-class performance through quality and innovation.

Our Mission

Empower organizations with practical tools, knowledge, and sustainable strategies.

Core Values

Integrity, innovation, client success, and continuous learning.

Our Story

Founded in 1998, QCI started as a small team of quality enthusiasts and has grown into an internationally recognized institute. We have trained over 10,000 professionals and consulted for more than 500 companies across South Asia, Middle East, and Europe.

"Be the best through process improvement – that's not just our tagline, it's our promise. Every client, every training, every solution is designed to elevate performance."

— Dr.Ijaz Yousaf, CEO & Founder
